BIDS AND AWARD COMMITTEE PROCUREMENT MANAGEMENT SYSTEM USER MANUAL


1. SYSTEM OVERVIEW

The Bids and Award Committee Procurement Management System is used to manage procurement documents, purchase requests, purchase orders, users, calendar activities, and procurement progress.

Main users of the system:

- BAC Secretariat (Administrator)
- BAC Member
- End User
- Supply Officer

3. LOGGING IN

1. Open the system in a web browser.
2. Enter your email or username.
3. Enter your password.
4. Click Login.

If the system asks you to change your password, enter a new password before continuing.

Password must have:

- At least 8 characters
- Uppercase and lowercase letters
- At least 1 number
- At least 1 special character

The system will automatically log out after 5 minutes of inactivity.


4. MAIN MENU

The sidebar menu shows the pages available to your role.

Common pages:

- Dashboard: View summaries, calendar activities, notifications, and procurement progress.
- Procurement Records: View procurement folders and uploaded documents.
- PPMP: Upload and view Project Procurement Management Plan documents.
- APP: Upload and view Annual Procurement Plan documents.
- Purchase Request: Create, view, assign, and download purchase requests.

Administrator pages:

- User Management: Add and update users.
- Activity Logs: View system activity history.

Supply Officer pages:

- Supply Dashboard: View purchase requests ready for PO.
- Purchase Orders: Create, view, and download purchase orders.


5. DASHBOARD

Use the Dashboard to:

- Check procurement statistics.
- View calendar activities.
- View procurement progress.
- Open notifications.
- Access account options and logout.

Administrators can add or edit calendar activities.


6. PPMP AND APP

Use the PPMP and APP pages to upload and view procurement planning documents.

To upload:

1. Open PPMP or APP from the sidebar.
2. Click Upload PPMP or Upload APP.
3. Fill in the required details.
4. Select the file.
5. Submit the form.

To view:

1. Open PPMP or APP.
2. Find the record in the table.
3. Click View.


7. PURCHASE REQUEST

Use the Purchase Request page to create and monitor PRs.

To create a PR:

1. Open Purchase Request.
2. Click New PR.
3. Fill in the required details and item list.
4. Review the total amount.
5. Save the PR.

To view or download:

1. Open Purchase Request.
2. Click View to see details.
3. Click Download to generate a PDF copy.

To assign a PR number:

1. Log in as a BAC Member.
2. Open Purchase Request.
3. Click Assign PR No. beside the record.
4. Enter the official PR number.
5. Save.


8. PROCUREMENT RECORDS

Use Procurement Records to view procurement folders and related documents.

To open a folder:

1. Open Procurement Records.
2. Click a procurement folder card.
3. Review the documents and workflow status.

Common statuses:

- ON GOING: The procurement is still being processed.
- COMPLETED: The procurement record is complete.


9. PURCHASE ORDERS

Supply Officers and Administrators use the Purchase Orders page to generate POs from completed PRs.

To create a PO:

1. Open the Supply Dashboard.
2. Find a PR under Pending Purchase Orders.
3. Click Process PO.
4. Fill in supplier and delivery details.
5. Preview the Purchase Order.
6. Click Confirm & Save.

To view or download a PO:

1. Open Purchase Orders.
2. Search by PO number, PR number, or supplier.
3. Click View or Download.


10. USER MANAGEMENT

Administrators use User Management to create and update accounts.

To add a user:

1. Open User Management.
2. Click Add User.
3. Enter the email, full name, role, and office.
4. Submit and confirm.
5. Copy the temporary password and give it securely to the user.

To edit a user:

1. Open User Management.
2. Click the edit button beside the user.
3. Update the details or account status.
4. Save the changes.


11. LOGOUT

To log out:

1. Open the account menu.
2. Click Logout.

Always log out after using the system, especially on shared computers.


12. BASIC TROUBLESHOOTING

Cannot log in:

- Check the username and password.
- Ask the administrator if the account is active.

File will not upload:

- Make sure all required fields are filled in.
- Make sure the correct file is selected.

Document preview does not open:

- Allow pop-ups in the browser.
- Try clicking View again.

PR number cannot be assigned:

- Make sure you are logged in as a BAC Member.
- Make sure the PR number is not already used.

PO cannot be generated:

- Make sure the PR is completed and has a PR number.
- Fill in all required PO fields.
